Indomie

Description

This is how you make Indomie

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Instant Indomie
  • Egg
  • Fried Shallots

Steps

  1. Boil 250ml of water into a pot
  2. Put the Instant Indomie into the water
  3. Wait for the noodles to loosen and put in the seasoning
  4. Add egg and fried shallots
  5. Eat!
